### `Latest`
# **Version 1.0**
Welcome to Notes to Reminders! Transform your Apple Notes checklists into actionable Reminders with just a few taps.

#### Features
- Share checklists directly from Apple Notes using the iOS share sheet
- Smart detection of completed and incomplete items
- Support for Apple Notes checklists and Markdown-style tasks
- Choose from existing Reminders lists or create new ones
- Preserves completion status and item order
- Privacy-focused: all data stays on your device
- Fast and intuitive interface
- Support for multiple checklist formats

#### Privacy
- No data collection
- No analytics or tracking
- Everything happens locally on your device
- [View Privacy Policy](/privacypolicy)
