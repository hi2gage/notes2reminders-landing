# Press Kit

## About Notes to Reminders

**Notes to Reminders** is a free iOS app that transforms Apple Notes checklists into actionable Reminders with just a few taps. Using the native iOS share sheet, users can instantly convert their to-do lists while preserving completion status and item organization.

### Key Features

- **Seamless Integration:** Uses iOS native share sheet for instant conversion
- **Smart Detection:** Automatically identifies completed and incomplete items
- **Flexible Lists:** Choose existing Reminders lists or create new ones on the fly
- **Multiple Formats:** Supports Apple Notes checklists and Markdown-style tasks
- **Privacy First:** All processing happens locally on device - no data collection
- **Fast & Simple:** Transform checklists in seconds

### Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**DraftRoom Studios Launches Notes to Reminders - The Fastest Way to Transform Apple Notes Checklists**

*Free iOS app brings seamless checklist conversion to the Apple ecosystem*

**November 2025** - DraftRoom Studios today announced the release of Notes to Reminders, a free iOS app that makes it effortless to convert Apple Notes checklists into actionable Reminders. With a focus on privacy and simplicity, the app integrates directly with iOS's native share sheet to provide instant checklist conversion.

Notes to Reminders bridges the gap between Apple Notes and Reminders, allowing users to quickly convert their to-do lists while maintaining complete privacy - everything happens on the device.

**Key features include:**

- One-tap sharing from Apple Notes using the iOS share sheet
- Smart detection that preserves completed and incomplete items
- Support for multiple checklist formats including Apple Notes and Markdown
- Ability to select existing Reminders lists or create new ones
- Zero data collection - all processing happens locally

Notes to Reminders is available now as a free download on the App Store for devices running iOS 18.6 or later.
